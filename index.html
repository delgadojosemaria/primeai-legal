Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Introduction

My Prime AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the My Prime AI application ("App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Photos: When you use the face scanning feature, the photo you select or take is temporarily processed to generate your analysis. Photos are not stored on our servers and are not retained after the analysis is complete.

Profile Information: We store locally on your device the name, gender, and age range you provide during onboarding. This information is used solely to personalise your experience.

Analytics Data: We collect anonymised usage data to understand how the App is used and to improve performance. This includes:

  • Whether the App has been installed (recorded once per device using an anonymous flag)
  • App open counts per device
  • Active session presence, including a partial device identifier (the first 8 characters of Apple's vendor identifier, which is app-specific and cannot identify you across apps), your iOS version, and your device's region setting
  • Whether a paid subscription or free trial was started

This data is stored in Firebase (Google) and does not include your name, photos, or any directly identifying information.

Purchase Information: Subscription and purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple and RevenueCat. We do not collect or store your payment details.

Face Data

My Prime AI processes facial images solely to provide AI-powered skin and feature analysis. The following explains exactly how face data is handled:

What face data is collected: The app processes photos that you voluntarily submit (taken with your camera or chosen from your photo library). No biometric identifiers, facial recognition templates, or facial geometry data are extracted or stored. The AI returns a text-based analysis only.

How face data is used: Your photo is transmitted securely over HTTPS to Anthropic's Claude API, which analyses the image and returns a text-based analysis of visible facial features such as skin quality, symmetry, and bone structure. The photo is used solely for this purpose.

Third-party sharing: Your photo is transmitted only to Anthropic (Claude API) to perform the analysis. It is not shared with any other third party, sold, or used for advertising purposes. Anthropic does not retain the photo after the API request completes. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy for details.

Storage: We do not store your photo on any server. After the analysis is complete, your most recent photo is optionally saved locally on your own device (in the app's private Documents directory) solely so it can be displayed within the app. This local copy never leaves your device.

Retention: The local copy of your photo is retained on your device until you perform a new scan (which replaces it) or delete the app (which removes all local data). No face data is retained on any external server.

User control: You may delete all locally stored data at any time by deleting the app from your device.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the face analysis service and generate your personalised improvement plan
  • To personalise your experience based on your profile (gender, age range)
  • To process and verify your subscription status
  • To improve and maintain the App
  • To comply with legal obligations

Third-Party Services

My Prime AI uses the following third-party services:

Anthropic Claude API: Your photo is securely transmitted to Anthropic's API to perform AI-based facial analysis. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this processing. Photos are not stored by Anthropic beyond the duration of the API request.

RevenueCat: Used to manage in-app subscriptions and purchase validation. RevenueCat may collect anonymised purchase and subscription data. See RevenueCat's privacy policy for details.

Apple App Store: All payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not access your payment information.

Firebase (Google): We use Firebase Firestore and Firebase Realtime Database to store anonymised analytics data (install counts, open counts, active sessions, and subscription events). Google's privacy policy applies to data processed through Firebase.

Data Storage and Security

Your profile information (name, gender, age range) and scan history are stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage mechanisms. We do not upload or store this data on external servers.

Photos you submit for analysis are transmitted securely over HTTPS and are not retained after the analysis response is received.

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

My Prime AI is not intended for users under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

You may delete all locally stored data at any time by deleting the App from your device. Since we do not store personal data on external servers, no additional deletion request is required.
